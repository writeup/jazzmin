TDJ Special Projects is an initiative created by Toronto Downtown Jazz (TDJ), producers of the annual TD Toronto Jazz Festival, with the support of JAZZ.FM91. The initiative encourages the creation, development and presentation of special projects by jazz musicians in the City of Toronto.
